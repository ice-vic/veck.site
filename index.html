Essential Controls

  • **WASD:** Movement.
  • **Mouse Left/Right:** Shoot and Aim Down Sight (ADS).
  • **Shift / C:** **Slide** (Your most important movement tool).
  • **Space:** Jump.
  • **R:** Reload.

Advanced Movement Tips

  • **The Slide-Jump:** Combine a slide ($\text{Shift}$) with a jump ($\text{Space}$) right before the slide ends to maintain maximum momentum and speed.
  • **Crosshair Placement:** Always keep your crosshair at head level, especially when turning corners. This shaves milliseconds off your reaction time.
  • **Pre-Aiming:** Anticipate where enemies will appear based on spawn points and map design. Never peek a corner without a plan.

Deep Dive: Fire Control and Strategy

In Veck.io, successful engagement relies on controlling your weapon's recoil. At long range, transition from full auto to short **bursts (3-5 rounds)** to ensure accuracy and minimal bullet spread. Reserve **full-auto sweeping** for close-quarters engagements where mobility (like slide-jumping around the target) is prioritized over pin-point accuracy. Furthermore, high-level play demands sound awareness‚Äîlisten closely for enemy footsteps and reload cues to anticipate attacks before the enemy even appears on your screen.
